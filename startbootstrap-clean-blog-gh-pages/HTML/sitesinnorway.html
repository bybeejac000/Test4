Details of the Hikes

  1. Lofoten
    • An island chain in Nordland
    • Contains all the hikes on this list
    • Mainly populated by fisherman

  2. Regnebringen
    • Located near the end of the Lofoten Islands
    • The most famous hike on Lofoten
    • Over 1,560 steps to the top

  3. Town of Regne
    • A small fishermans village
    • Has extreme weather and has rain much of the year
